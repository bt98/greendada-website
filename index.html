About Us

Our main purpose in Green Dada is to increase the number of Adolescents girls and young women who have relevant skills including technology and entrepreneurship for sustainability.

Our Background.

Our Inspiration is dawn from the need to foster Gender Equality evidently seen during the covid 19 pandemic which amplified the existing of Gender Inequalities posing unique threats to Girls and young women's livelihood, health and safety in kenya and the world.

Our Mission

To fostering collective action to tackle inequalities faced by Adolescents girls and young women in all their diversities by raising awareness through Education.

Our Vision

A Healthier, Happier and Greener Society.
